Ethwei is a Layer 1 blockchain purpose-engineered for real world asset
tokenization. Bring property, commodities, credit, and equity on-chain with
enterprise-grade security.

A boring and simple blockchain is a good blockchain. We believe the
infrastructure that moves trillions in real world value should be predictable,
auditable, and dependable — not flashy.

Stay tuned as we roll out our testnet, built on the Cosmos SDK.
