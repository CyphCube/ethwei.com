Introducing Ethwei: A Boring Blockchain for Real World Assets

June 15, 2026

Ethwei is a Layer 1 blockchain purpose-engineered for real world asset tokenization. Bring property, commodities, credit, and equity on-chain with enterprise-grade security.

A boring and simple blockchain is a good blockchain. We believe the infrastructure that moves trillions in real world value should be predictable, auditable, and dependable — not flashy.
